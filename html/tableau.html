California is the most profitable state by far, followed by New York, and then Washington. No sales data exists for the states of Wyoming and Maine.



California profits have continued to increase over the past two years, with seasonal dips in the first half of the year and peaks in the second half.



Office supplies are the most profitable category in California, with leading sub-categories being binders, appliances, paper, and storage.



Data is somewhat sparse, but California profits are projected to continue their current trend over the next 11 months.
